🍲 INDONESIAN 🥩 MAIN COURSE

Rendang Sapi
Padang Autentik

Discover the soul of Minangkabau cuisine. A slow-cooked masterpiece of beef, coconut milk, and a symphony of spices reduced to perfection over hours of patient simmering.

Ingredients

For 4 px

SPICE PASTE

Master Class

Visual learner? Watch our chef's mastery.

The Crafting Process

01

Aromatic Foundation

In a high-speed blender or traditional mortar, process shallots, garlic, chilies, ginger, galangal, and turmeric until a vibrant, smooth paste emerges. This "bumbu" is the soul of the dish.

02

Infusion Ceremony

Combine coconut milk and spice paste in a wide wok. Add in lemongrass, lime leaves, and turmeric leaves. Bring to a low boil, whisking constantly to ensure the coconut fats emulsify perfectly with the aromatics.

03

The Slow Reduction

Introduce the beef. Reduce heat to a whisper. Let it simmer for 3 hours. Watch as the ivory liquid transforms into a rich orange gravy (kalio), then slowly darkens as the coconut milk renders its oils.

04

The Caramelization

In the final hour, stir constantly. The moisture vanishes, leaving the beef to fry in its own aromatic oil. The sugars in the coconut milk caramelize to a deep, chocolate brown. This is the hallmark of true Rendang.
